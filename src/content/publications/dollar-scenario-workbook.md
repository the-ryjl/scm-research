## Why use scenarios?

Currency narratives are seductive because they can be made to explain almost any move after the fact. A scenario workbook creates discipline by stating the proposed transmission mechanism in advance.

## Illustrative matrix

| Scenario | Relative growth | Rate differential | Risk appetite | Illustrative dollar pressure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A | US stronger | Wider | Stable | Upward |
| B | Converging | Narrower | Stable | Downward |
| C | Global shock | Mixed | Weak | Ambiguous / safe-haven dependent |

## Monitoring the mechanism

Each scenario should name the data that would confirm it, the lag we expect, and the observation that would falsify it. That turns a macro story into a research process rather than a permanent opinion.

## Classroom takeaway

The point is not to eliminate uncertainty. It is to show where uncertainty lives and prevent one data release from silently changing the entire explanation.
